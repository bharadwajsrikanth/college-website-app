Computer science and engineering(C.S.E)

Bangalore Institute Of technology was a pioneer to introduce Computer Science and Engineering as an engineering branch in Karnataka. It was formed in 1979 and has since been one of the top branches not only in BIT but also in Karnataka. BIT is above other other institutes is mainly because of this department. It has always got top ranks in the university and has been consistent ever since its establishment.

Prof Dr.S.Nandagopalan(M.sc,M.Tech,M.S,Ph.D.) is not only a the Head of the department of computer science and engineering but is also an influential and dynamic figure in the department. Being a humble person, has always looked at betterment of the students and of the department. He has been teaching CS for the past 28 years.
